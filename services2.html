HYTEL NW PREMIUM VOICE

HyTEL NW “Premium voice” is a complete voice solution, offering a comprehensive range of inbound and outbound voice services at highly competitive rates, offers pure per second billing for clients with larger bills – matching Telkom’s strategy at a lower tariff. Home, SME, Corporate.

FEATURES

  • Customized outbound rate plan
  • Geographic number range ( eg. 018, 011, 021, 031, 041, etc )
  • Complete Infrastructure solution
  • Use of carrier grade equipment, specified bandwidth and direct interconnects with all first Class Tier Telco’s
  • Remote service management of call routing

BENIFITS

  • Cost effective voice solution, with numbers that reflect location
  • Increase savings dramatically with constant savings on outbound calls
  • Consistent access and high quality calls
  • Account management
  • Online itemized automated billing
  • Quick response and fault resolution times

NETWORK LAYOUT

Both Voice and Internet traffic is transported from the customer premises (CPE) over the HYTEL wireless network Both Voice and Data back hauls to the HYTEL SIP Server (VOIP) and Radius Internet server Using First Tier Carrier partners, voice and data traffic interconnects with Telkom, Neotel, Vodacom, MTN and Cell C, whilst Internet traffic connects to Seacom and SAIX.
